Bridging Physical and Digital.

We started Auxmatica because we noticed a disconnect. Live events were incredible, but the digital tools supporting them were clunky, distracting, and disconnected from the environment.

Our philosophy is simple: Technology should enhance your presence in the real world, not pull you out of it. We build systems that are intuitive, beautiful, and hyper-aware of their surroundings.
